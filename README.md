# mbah-divine # Maven Marketing Campaign Analysis

## Overview

This README summarizes insights from the Maven Marketing Campaign dataset, focusing on customer behavior, factors influencing web purchases, campaign effectiveness, and overall customer profile characteristics. The analysis is based on a filtered dataset of **160 customers**.

---

## Key Factors Related to Web Purchases

The analysis identified several variables significantly associated with online purchase behavior:

### **1. Income**

* Higher-income customers tend to make more web purchases.
* Indicates stronger digital purchasing power and comfort with online transactions.

### **2. Age**

* Younger customers purchase online more frequently.
* Suggests that digital engagement decreases with increasing age.

### **3. Education Level**

* Education has a notable impact.
* **Graduate** and **Master's degree** holders show more online activity.
* Customers with higher education may be more tech-savvy and inclined toward digital shopping.

### **4. Household Composition**

* Customers without children at home show higher online activity.
* May indicate greater disposable time or resources for online shopping.

---

## Marketing Campaign Performance

### **Most Successful Campaigns**

* **Campaign 4** had the highest acceptance among individual campaigns with **167 acceptances**.
* The **cumulative final campaign** (represented by the *Response* column) had **334 acceptances**, making it the **most successful initiative overall**.

## Average Customer Profile (Filtered Dataset)

Based on the 160‑customer dataset:

### **Demographics**

* **Average Age:** 57.5 years
* **Median Income:** 51,000
* **Education Breakdown:**

  * Graduate: 53.13%
  * Master's: 18.75%
  * PhD: 20%

### **Commonly Purchased Products**

Customers most frequently purchased:

* **Wines**
* **Meat products**
* **Gold products**

These categories represent the highest spending and product engagement within the dataset.

---

## Summary of Findings

* Online purchasing is strongly influenced by **income**, **age**, **education**, and **household composition**.
* **Campaign 4** performed best individually, while the **final cumulative campaign** was the most successful overall.
* The average customer is a **middle‑aged, educated individual with mid‑range income**, purchasing primarily premium consumables and gold products.
